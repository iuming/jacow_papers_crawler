FRXCA1
Passive Streaking Using Transverse Wakefield for Ultrashort Bunch Diagnostics
 


 

S. Bettoni
                       PSI, Villigen PSI, Switzerland

 


 

This talk will describe the experimental demonstration of the use of transverse wakes to passively streak a relativistic electron beam. The reconstruction method used to recover the temporal profile of the electron bunch will be presented and the limitations of the technique as a diagnostics discussed.
S. Bettoni, et al. PHYSICAL REVIEW ACCELERATORS AND BEAMS 19, 021304 (2016)
