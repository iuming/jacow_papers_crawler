FRTB1
Femtosecond Timing and Synchronization of Laser Systems for Accelerators
 


 

J.C. Frisch
                       SLAC, Menlo Park, California, USA

 


 

This tutorial should describe the challenges and demands in timing and synchronization for accelerators, with an emphasis on femtosecond timing of laser systems. The tutorial should describe the wide variety of timing and laser timing challenges and system approaches.
