FR3A01
Dielectric Linear Accelerator - Accelerator on a Chip
 


 

R.J. England
                       SLAC, Menlo Park, California, USA

 


 

DLA - the accelerator on a chip - offers a paradigm for accelerators where they would be manufactured as semiconductor wafers and use industrially produced lasers as power sources. The recent acknowledgement with a 13 M$ grant from the Moore Foundation will advance the technology. Recent results and future plans will be shown.
