Opening & Welcome
 


 

A.J. Roque da Silva, H. Westfahl Jr.

The CNPEM Director General Antonio José Roque da Silva and the LNLS Director Harry Westfahl Jr. welcome you to the 9th International Beam Instrumentation Conference. Watch a tour through CNPEM’s four national laboratories and take a look inside Sirius’s storage ring tunnel and beamlines under commissioning and under construction.
