FRYBA01
Recent LHC Physics Results and their Impact on Future HEP Accelerator Programme
 


 

S. Bertolucci
                       CERN, Geneva, Switzerland

 


 

This presentation will review the exciting results obtained by the LHC experiments at CERN for the proton and ion physics programmes, and describe the impact of these results on the physics landscape and implications for future accelerator-based high energy physics research.
