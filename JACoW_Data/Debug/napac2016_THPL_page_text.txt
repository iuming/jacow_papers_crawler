THPLIO01
What Life Is Like as a Scientist in Congress
 


 

G.W. Foster
                       Fermilab, Batavia, Illinois, USA

 


 

Bill Foster has given several excellent talks pointing out that scientists and engineers are greatly underrepresented in politics, and that the people making decisions about science funding do not have adequate background to make informed decisions themselves. He has excellent suggestions in the talk as to how we can contribute.
